Vegan Split Pea Soup

Description

A hearty, thick soup filled with split peas, carrots, celery, and potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tblsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 7 1/2 cups Water
  • 2 cups Dried Split Peas
  • 1/2 cup Barley
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3 Potatoes, diced
  • 3 Carrots, chopped
  • 3 stalks Celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Parsely, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1/2 tsp Gound Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather all of the ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in large pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Saute onion, garlic, and bay leaf in hot oil until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add water, peas, baley, and salt; bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add potatoes, carrots, celery, parsley, basil, thyme, and pepper. Simmer until peas and vegetables are tender, about 1 hour.
